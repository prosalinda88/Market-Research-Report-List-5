<p><p>ECG lead wires are commonly made from different materials such as TPE, TPU, silicone, and PVC. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) wires are known for their flexibility, durability, and resistance to chemicals. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) wires offer similar properties to TPE but with added abrasion resistance. Silicone wires are known for their biocompatibility, flexibility, and ease of cleaning. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) wires are cost-effective and widely used, though they may not be as flexible or durable as the other options.</p></p>
