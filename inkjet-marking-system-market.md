<p><p>Inkjet marking systems are commonly divided into two main market types: Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) and Drop-on-Demand (DOD). CIJ systems are ideal for high-speed applications where quick drying time is essential, such as printing expiration dates on products. DOD systems, on the other hand, are better suited for smaller-scale applications that require precise control over droplet placement, such as printing barcodes or serial numbers on packaging. Both market types offer a range of features and capabilities to suit different marking needs.</p></p>
