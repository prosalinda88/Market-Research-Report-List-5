<p><p>Dissecting microscopes are commonly found in two main types: binocular microscopes and trinocular microscopes. Binocular microscopes have two eyepieces, allowing for simultaneous use by both eyes and providing a wider field of view. Trinocular microscopes, on the other hand, have three eyepieces, with the third typically used for attaching a camera or other imaging device. Both types of microscopes are commonly used in biological and medical research, as well as in educational settings.</p></p>
