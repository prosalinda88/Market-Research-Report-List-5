<p><p>Hydraulic vane pump market consists of two main types: Variable Pump and Quantitative Pump. Variable Pump allows for adjustable flow rates and pressure output, making it suitable for applications where varying hydraulic power is required. On the other hand, Quantitative Pump delivers a constant flow rate regardless of changes in pressure, making it ideal for applications where consistent power output is essential. Both types cater to different hydraulic system requirements, providing flexibility and reliability in various industrial settings.</p></p>
