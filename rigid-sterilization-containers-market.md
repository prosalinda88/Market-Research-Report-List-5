<p><p>Rigid sterilization containers come in two main types: Filter Type and Valve Type. Filter Type containers have built-in filters that allow steam to enter and escape during the sterilization process while preventing bacteria from entering afterwards. Valve Type containers, on the other hand, have valves that control the airflow during sterilization, ensuring proper conditions for sterilization. Both types are commonly used in healthcare facilities to safely sterilize and store medical instruments.</p></p>
