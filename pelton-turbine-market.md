<p><ul><li>Single Nozzle</li><li>Twin-Nozzle</li><li>Multi-Nozzle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pelton turbines are commonly classified into three market types based on the number of nozzles used: Single Nozzle, Twin-Nozzle, and Multi-Nozzle markets. Single nozzle markets feature turbines that have only one water inlet nozzle, while twin-nozzle markets have two nozzles with the water jet hitting in the middle. Multi-nozzle markets have more than two nozzles on a single turbine, allowing for a higher flow rate and increased power output. Each market type offers different advantages in terms of efficiency and performance.</p></p>
