<p><p>Automotive Electrical Park Brake (EPB) systems can be categorized into two types based on the presence of Auto Hold functionality. In vehicles with Auto Hold, the system automatically applies the brakes when the vehicle comes to a stop, allowing the driver to release the brake pedal. In vehicles without Auto Hold, the driver must manually apply and release the brakes when the vehicle is stationary. Both systems are designed to enhance convenience and safety, with Auto Hold offering an additional level of automation.</p></p>
