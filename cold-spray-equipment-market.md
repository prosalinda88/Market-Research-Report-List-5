<p><strong>The Cold Spray Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Pressure Cold Spray (HPCS)</li><li>Low Pressure Cold Spray (LPCS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold spray equipment market is segmented into High Pressure Cold Spray (HPCS) and Low Pressure Cold Spray (LPCS) types. HPCS involves using gas pressures above 50 bar to accelerate the powder particles, resulting in high impact energy and improved deposition efficiency. On the other hand, LPCS operates at lower gas pressures, typically below 10 bar, making it suitable for more delicate or thin substrates. Both types offer unique advantages and applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
