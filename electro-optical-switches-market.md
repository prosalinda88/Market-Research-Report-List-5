<p><p>Electro-optical switches refer to devices that use electrical signals to control the routing of optical signals. In the market, there are two main types of electro-optical switches: single-mode switches and multimode switches. Single-mode switches are designed to work with a single mode fiber, which allows for long-distance, high-speed communication. On the other hand, multimode switches are used with multimode fibers, which are better suited for shorter distance, lower speed applications. Both types of switches play a crucial role in telecommunications and data networks.</p></p>
