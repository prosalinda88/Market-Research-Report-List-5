<p><strong>The Audio Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Output Transformer</li><li>Input Transformer</li><li>Interstage Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio transformers can be categorized into three main types based on their functions: output transformers, input transformers, and interstage transformers. </p><p>Output transformers are used to match the impedance of the amplifier to the speaker load, ensuring efficient power transfer. Input transformers are employed to match the impedance of the source signal to the input stage of the amplifier, maintaining signal integrity. Interstage transformers are utilized to transfer signals between amplifier stages, providing isolation and impedance matching for optimal signal transmission. Each type plays a crucial role in the audio system for high-quality sound reproduction.</p></p>
