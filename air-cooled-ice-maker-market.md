<p><p>Air Cooled Ice Makers are divided into two main types: Air Cooled Full Cube Ice Machines and Air Cooled Half Cube Ice Machines. Full cube ice machines produce larger, solid cubes that melt slowly, making them ideal for drinks that need to stay cold longer. On the other hand, half cube ice machines produce smaller, more porous cubes that are easier to chew and therefore popular in soft drinks and cocktails. Both types are air-cooled, meaning they use air to cool down the ice-making process.</p></p>
