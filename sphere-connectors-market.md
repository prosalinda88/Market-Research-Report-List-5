<p><ul><li>Single Sphere Connector</li><li>Twin Sphere Connector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single Sphere Connectors are designed with only one sphere to absorb movement and vibration in piping systems. They are commonly used in applications where limited movement is expected. Twin Sphere Connectors have two spheres, providing greater flexibility and movement absorption. They are ideal for applications with higher levels of movement and vibration. Both types of connectors are used in a variety of industries such as HVAC, plumbing, and industrial applications to prevent damage to pipelines and equipment from stress and movement.</p></p>
