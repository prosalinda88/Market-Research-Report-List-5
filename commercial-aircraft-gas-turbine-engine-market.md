<p><p>The Commercial Aircraft Gas Turbine Engine market can be classified into two main types based on the bypass ratio: High-Bypass Type and Low-Bypass Type. High-Bypass Type engines have a larger bypass ratio, which means a higher percentage of air bypasses the core engine compared to the Low-Bypass Type. These engines are more fuel efficient and quieter, making them suitable for long-haul passenger flights. On the other hand, Low-Bypass Type engines have a lower bypass ratio and are typically used in military aircraft or smaller commercial planes that require higher thrust-to-weight ratios.</p></p>
