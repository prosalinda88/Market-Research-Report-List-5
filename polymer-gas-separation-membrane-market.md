<p><p>Polymer gas separation membranes come in various types, with the three main categories being hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes consist of a bundle of small tubes, offering high surface area for gas separation. Spiral wound membranes are composed of multiple layers wound in a spiral configuration, providing efficient separation of gases. Other types of membranes include asymmetric and composite membranes, each offering unique advantages in gas separation applications. These diverse membrane options cater to different industrial needs for gas separation processes.</p></p>
