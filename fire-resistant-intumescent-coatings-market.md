<p><p>Fire resistant intumescent coatings are used to protect materials from fire by expanding and forming a protective char layer when exposed to heat. These coatings are available in three different market types: spray, brush, and roller. Spray applications are typically used for larger surface areas and provide a quick and even coverage. Brush coatings offer precision and are suitable for smaller areas or touch-ups. Roller applications provide a balance between speed and precision, making them ideal for medium-sized projects.</p></p>
