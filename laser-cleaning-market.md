<p><p>Laser cleaning is a process that uses laser beams to remove contaminants, oxides, rust, paint, or other unwanted materials from a surface. It is a non-contact and environmentally friendly method compared to traditional cleaning methods that involve chemicals or abrasives. The laser beam evaporates the contaminants without damaging the underlying substrate, making it a preferred choice in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, electronics, and cultural heritage conservation.</p><p>The global laser cleaning market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.7% during the forecast period. The increasing adoption of laser cleaning technology in industrial applications, the demand for eco-friendly cleaning methods, and the growing emphasis on precision cleaning are driving the market growth. Additionally, advancements in laser technology, such as the development of ultrafast lasers and fiber lasers, are further fueling market expansion.</p><p>One of the key trends in the laser cleaning market is the integration of automation and robotics. The combination of laser cleaning with robotic systems allows for efficient and precise cleaning in complex and hard-to-reach areas.
