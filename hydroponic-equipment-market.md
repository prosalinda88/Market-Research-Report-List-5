Hydroponic Drip System involves delivering nutrient-rich water directly to plant roots. Aeroponic System uses a misting system to deliver nutrients. Ebb and Flow Hydroponic System intermittently floods plant roots with nutrient solution. Deep Water Culture System involves suspending plant roots in a nutrient solution.
