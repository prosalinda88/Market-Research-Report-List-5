<p><p>The Low Power IoT market consists of three main types: NB-IoT, LoRa, and LTE-M. NB-IoT, or Narrowband IoT, uses existing cellular networks to enable low-power, wide-area communication for IoT devices. LoRa technology utilizes long-range, low-power wireless communication to connect IoT devices. LTE-M, or Long-Term Evolution for Machines, is a low-power network technology specifically designed for IoT applications. Each market type offers unique advantages in terms of range, power consumption, and compatibility, catering to the diverse needs of the IoT industry.</p></p>
