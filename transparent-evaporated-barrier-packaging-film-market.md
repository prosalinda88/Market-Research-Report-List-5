<p><strong>The Transparent Evaporated Barrier Packaging Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AlOx Type</li><li>SiOx Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transparent evaporated barrier packaging films are classified into two main types: AlOx type and SiOx type. AlOx type films are coated with aluminum oxide, providing high barrier properties against moisture and oxygen. On the other hand, SiOx type films are coated with silicon oxide, offering similar barrier properties but with improved transparency. Both types of films are commonly used in food and pharmaceutical packaging to extend shelf life and maintain product quality by preventing exposure to external factors like moisture and oxygen.</p></p>
