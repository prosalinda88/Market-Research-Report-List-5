<p><strong>The Rayon Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Viscose Staple Fiber</li><li>Viscose Filament Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rayon fibers can be divided into two main market types: Viscose staple fiber and Viscose filament fiber. Viscose staple fiber is a short-cut version of rayon, having short fibers spun into yarn and then woven into fabrics. It is commonly used in clothing, upholstery, and non-woven applications. On the other hand, Viscose filament fiber is a long continuous thread of rayon used in textiles, industrial applications, and packaging materials. Both types of rayon fibers offer versatility, softness, and drapability in various end products.</p></p>
