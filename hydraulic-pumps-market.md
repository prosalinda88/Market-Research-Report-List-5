<p><p>Hydraulic pumps market can be categorized into different types based on their designs and functioning. The main types include hydraulic gear pumps, hydraulic vane pumps, hydraulic piston pumps, and others. Hydraulic gear pumps use meshing gears to pump fluid, hydraulic vane pumps utilize vanes mounted to a rotor to move fluid, and hydraulic piston pumps use pistons to pressurize fluid. Other types may include screw pumps, peristaltic pumps, and radial pumps, each offering unique features and applications in various industries.</p></p>
