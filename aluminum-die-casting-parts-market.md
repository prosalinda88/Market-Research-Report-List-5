<p><ul><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Pressure Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum die casting parts market is segmented into two main types: vacuum die casting and pressure die casting. Vacuum die casting involves removing air and gases from the mold before the molten metal is injected, resulting in higher quality and fewer defects. On the other hand, pressure die casting uses significant pressure to force the molten metal into the mold cavity quickly, leading to faster production speeds and cost efficiency. Both methods are widely used in various industries for manufacturing aluminum parts.</p></p>
