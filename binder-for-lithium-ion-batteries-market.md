<p><p>Anode binders and cathode binders are both crucial components in lithium-ion batteries, serving to hold active materials in place and facilitate electron flow within the battery. Anode binders are used on the negative electrode side, providing stability and conductivity for the graphite-based material. Cathode binders, on the other hand, are used on the positive electrode side, helping to maintain the structural integrity of the lithium-based material. Both types of binders play essential roles in the overall performance and efficiency of lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
