<p><p>Gynecological operating tables come in two main types: height-adjustable and fixed-height. Height-adjustable tables can be easily adjusted to the desired height, making them suitable for various procedures and ensuring ergonomic working conditions for the medical staff. On the other hand, fixed-height tables have a set height and are more stable during surgeries. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the healthcare facility and the procedures performed on the table.</p></p>
