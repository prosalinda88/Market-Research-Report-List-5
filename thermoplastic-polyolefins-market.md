<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyolefins (TPO) are widely used in various industries and can be classified into three main types - H Type, L Type, and P Type. H Type TPOs are high crystallinity materials that offer high tensile strength and impact resistance. L Type TPOs have low crystallinity and are flexible, making them suitable for applications requiring flexibility. P Type TPOs are a blend of H and L types, providing a balance of strength and flexibility. Each type caters to different applications, offering specific properties to meet the needs of various industries.</p></p>
