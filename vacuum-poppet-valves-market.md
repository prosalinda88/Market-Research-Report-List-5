<p><p>Vacuum poppet valves are used to control the flow of gases or liquids in vacuum systems. There are three main types of vacuum poppet valves: Angle Valve, Z-inline Valve, and Y-inline Valve. </p><p>Angle valves are designed to be mounted at a right angle to the vacuum chamber, Z-inline valves have a vertical body with the inlet and outlet on the same plane, and Y-inline valves have a horizontal body with the inlet and outlet at different angles. Each type of valve is suited for different applications and installation configurations in vacuum systems.</p></p>
