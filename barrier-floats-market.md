<p><p>Barrier floats are divided into two main types: round type and oval line type. The round type is characterized by a circular design, providing a stable and buoyant barrier for water bodies. Both types of barrier floats serve the purpose of demarcating areas in lakes, rivers, and other bodies of water for safety and navigational purposes.</p></p>
