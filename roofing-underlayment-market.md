<p><strong>The Roofing Underlayment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Bitumen Synthetic</li><li>Asphalt-Saturated Felt</li><li>Rubberized Asphalt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roofing underlayment market types include non-bitumen synthetic, asphalt-saturated felt, and rubberized asphalt. Non-bitumen synthetic underlayment is made from polypropylene or polyester and provides excellent protection against harsh weather conditions. Asphalt-saturated felt underlayment is made from organic or fiberglass materials and is cost-effective, but less durable than synthetic options. Rubberized asphalt underlayment is made from a blend of asphalt and rubber polymers, offering superior waterproofing and flexibility. Each type has its own benefits and is suitable for different roofing applications.</p></p>
