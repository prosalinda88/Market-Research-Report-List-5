<p><strong>The Electronic Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Pass Filter</li><li>High-Pass Filter</li><li>Band-Pass Filter</li><li>Band-Stop Filter</li><li>All-Pass Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic filters are used to allow certain frequencies to pass through while blocking others. The Low Pass Filter allows frequencies below a certain cutoff point to pass through. The High-Pass Filter allows frequencies above a certain cutoff point to pass through. The Band-Pass Filter allows a specific range of frequencies to pass through. The Band-Stop Filter, also known as a notch filter, blocks a specific range of frequencies. The All-Pass Filter allows all frequencies to pass through, but may alter the phase relationship between them.</p></p>
