<p><p>High strength concrete is classified based on its compressive strength, with three main types: C60-C80, C80-C100, and above C100. C60-C80 concrete typically has a compressive strength ranging from 60 to 80 MPa, while C80-C100 has a range of 80 to 100 MPa. Concrete with a compressive strength above C100 exceeds 100 MPa. These high strength concrete types are commonly used in applications where superior durability, stiffness, and load-bearing capacity are required, such as in high-rise buildings, bridges, and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
