<p><p>Porous membrane filters are categorized into several types based on the material they are made from. The types include inorganic membrane filters, which offer high chemical and temperature resistance. PVDF filters have excellent solvent resistance and are suitable for a wide range of applications. PTFE filters have high chemical compatibility and are commonly used in critical filtration applications. PES filters are known for their low protein binding properties. Other types of porous membrane filters include cellulose acetate, nylon, and polypropylene filters which offer unique performance characteristics for specific filtration needs.</p></p>
