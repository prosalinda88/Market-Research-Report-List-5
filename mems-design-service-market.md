<p><p>MEMS Design Service Market offers two main types: Variable Capacitive Type and Piezoresistive Type. </p><p>Variable Capacitive Type utilizes changes in capacitance to detect physical quantities, making it suitable for pressure sensors and accelerometers. </p><p>Piezoresistive Type, on the other hand, uses changes in resistance due to strain for applications like force sensors and strain gauges. </p><p>Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different applications in the MEMS industry.</p></p>
