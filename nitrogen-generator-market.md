<p><p>Nitrogen generators are used to produce high-purity nitrogen gas for various industrial applications. There are two main types of nitrogen generators: PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) and membrane generators. PSA nitrogen generators separate nitrogen molecules from other gases using adsorption technology, while membrane generators use a semi-permeable membrane to separate nitrogen from other gases based on size differences. Both types are cost-effective and energy-efficient solutions for generating nitrogen gas on-site, eliminating the need for traditional nitrogen storage and delivery systems.</p></p>
