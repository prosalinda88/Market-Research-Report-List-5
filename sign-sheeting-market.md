<p><ul><li>Glass bead type</li><li>Micro prismatic type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sign sheeting market consists of two main types: glass bead type and micro prismatic type. Glass bead type sheeting incorporates tiny glass beads that reflect light, making signs visible in low light conditions. On the other hand, micro prismatic type sheeting consists of small, prism-like structures that reflect and refract light, enhancing visibility at long distances. Both types are crucial for ensuring road safety and effective communication through signage. Glass bead type is more common in temporary signage, while micro prismatic type is preferred for permanent applications.</p></p>
