<p><p>The Solar Powered Data Buoy Market is segmented into Base and Tower types. Base market data buoys are fixed to the sea floor, providing stable and accurate data collection. They are suitable for monitoring oceanographic parameters in deep waters. Tower market data buoys are designed to float on the water's surface, making them ideal for shallow water applications. They are equipped with sensors to measure various parameters such as temperature, salinity, and wave heights. Both types of data buoys rely on solar power for their energy needs.</p></p>
