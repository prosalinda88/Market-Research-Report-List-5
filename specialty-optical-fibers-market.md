<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode Specialty Optical Fiber</li><li>Single-Mode Specialty Optical Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Specialty optical fibers can be categorized into two main types: multi-mode and single-mode. Multi-mode specialty optical fibers are designed to carry multiple light rays simultaneously, making them ideal for short-distance communication systems. On the other hand, single-mode specialty optical fibers are designed to carry a single light ray, allowing for longer-distance communication with higher speeds and lower loss. Both types of specialty optical fibers are essential components in various industries such as telecommunications, medical devices, and sensors.</p></p>
