<p><strong>The Connected (Smart) Street Lights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wired (Power Line Communication)</li><li>Wireless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connected street lights can be classified into two main types: Wired (Power Line Communication) and Wireless. </p><p>Wired street lights use power line communication technology to transmit data through existing power lines, offering a reliable and stable connection. </p><p>Wireless street lights, on the other hand, do not require physical connections and instead use wireless communication protocols such as LoRa, Zigbee, or Bluetooth to transmit data. This makes installation easier and more cost-effective, especially in areas where laying down cables is not feasible. </p><p>Both types offer remote monitoring and control capabilities, improving efficiency and reducing maintenance costs.</p></p>
