<p><ul><li>Copper Braid Shield</li><li>Foil Shield</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data transmission cables come in different types based on the shielding used to protect the signal from interference. Copper braid shield cables use a woven mesh of copper strands to block electromagnetic interference, making them suitable for high-frequency signals. Foil shield cables, on the other hand, use a thin sheet of aluminum or copper foil to provide protection against interference. Both types are commonly used in networking and telecommunications applications where reliable data transmission is essential.</p></p>
