<p><p>Apex locators are devices used in endodontic treatment to accurately determine the length of the root canal. They can be categorized into two market types: Alarm Type and Digital Readout. </p><p>Alarm Type apex locators alert the user with an audible alarm when the tip reaches the apex of the root. This provides a simple and straightforward indication of the canal's length.</p><p>Digital Readout apex locators display the measurement digitally on a screen, allowing for precise and easy readings of the root canal length. This type offers a more detailed and accurate measurement compared to alarm type apex locators.</p></p>
