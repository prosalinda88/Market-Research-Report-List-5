<p><p>Automatic straightener machines come in two main types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic. Semi-automatic machines are often more cost-effective and suitable for smaller-scale operations, while fully-automatic machines offer higher efficiency and precision for larger-scale industrial applications. Both types of machines play a crucial role in various industries by straightening materials such as metal rods, pipes, and wires.</p></p>
