<p><p>The Marine Steering System market consists of three main types - Electronic Power Steering, Hydraulic Steering, and Mechanical Steering. Electronic Power Steering utilizes electrical power for steering control, offering precision and ease of use. Hydraulic Steering systems use hydraulic fluid to transmit steering force, providing smooth operation and strong control. Mechanical Steering systems rely on mechanical linkage to turn the boat's rudder, offering a simple and cost-effective steering solution. These different types cater to various needs and preferences of boat owners in the marine industry.</p></p>
