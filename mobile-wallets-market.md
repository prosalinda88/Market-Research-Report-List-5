<p><p>Mobile wallets are a type of payment method that allows users to securely store their credit card or debit card information on their mobile device. There are two main types of mobile wallets: remote payment and NFC. Remote payment wallets, such as PayPal or Google Pay, allow users to make online purchases without entering their payment information each time. NFC wallets, like Apple Pay or Samsung Pay, use near-field communication technology to allow users to make contactless payments in-store by simply tapping their phone on a compatible terminal.</p></p>
