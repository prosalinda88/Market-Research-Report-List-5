<p><p>Hydraulic Magnetic Circuit Breakers are available in two main types - Single-pole and Multi-pole. Single-pole breakers are designed to protect one circuit and can be used in applications where only one circuit needs to be disconnected in case of an overload. On the other hand, Multi-pole breakers are capable of protecting multiple circuits simultaneously, making them suitable for applications where multiple circuits need to be disconnected together. Both types provide reliable protection against overloads and short circuits, ensuring the safety of electrical systems.</p></p>
