<p><p>Data center energy storage market involves various types of technologies. Batteries are the most common form of energy storage, providing reliable and scalable power backup. Super capacitors offer quick bursts of energy for critical applications. Flywheels store energy through kinetic motion, providing rapid response times. Compressed Air Energy Storage (CAES) stores energy by compressing air in underground caverns. Other types include thermal storage, pumped hydro and hydrogen storage, offering diverse solutions for energy storage needs in data centers.</p></p>
