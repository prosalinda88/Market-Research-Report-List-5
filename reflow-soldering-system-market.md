<p><strong>The Reflow Soldering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Reflow Soldering</li><li>Condensation Reflow Soldering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflow Soldering System Market is segmented into two main types: Convection Reflow Soldering and Condensation Reflow Soldering. Convection Reflow Soldering uses a forced hot air convection process to melt and reflow the solder paste, making it popular for its efficiency and precise control. On the other hand, Condensation Reflow Soldering uses a two-stage process involving condensation of vaporized solvent before reflow, offering advantages such as reduced voiding and improved solder joint reliability. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements within the soldering industry.</p></p>
