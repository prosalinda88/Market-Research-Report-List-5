<p><p>Barrier shrink film is classified into four different types based on their barrier properties and strength levels: Low, Medium, High, and Ultra-high. </p><p>Low barrier shrink film offers basic protection against moisture and oxygen. Medium barrier shrink film provides better protection against external elements and is suitable for short-term storage. High barrier shrink film offers superior barrier properties and is ideal for long-term storage and protecting products from extreme conditions. Ultra-high barrier shrink film provides the highest level of protection against moisture, oxygen, and other contaminants, making it suitable for highly sensitive products that require extended shelf life.</p></p>
