<p><p>Dental radiology equipment market includes various types such as intraoral X-ray systems, extraoral X-ray systems, intraoral plate scanners, and cone-beam computed tomography (CBCT) imaging. Intraoral X-ray systems are used inside the mouth to capture detailed images of individual teeth and surrounding tissues. Extraoral X-ray systems capture images from outside the mouth for a broader view. Intraoral plate scanners produce digital images of X-ray films. CBCT imaging provides 3D images of the oral and maxillofacial region for more comprehensive diagnoses.</p></p>
