<p><ul><li>Heat pump dryers</li><li>Condenser dryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat pump dryers and condenser dryers are two types of electric dryers on the market. Heat pump dryers use a heat pump to recycle and reuse hot air, making them more energy-efficient and cost-effective over time. Condenser dryers, on the other hand, use a condenser to remove moisture from the air inside the dryer, making them more suitable for homes without proper ventilation. Both types of dryers offer different features and benefits, catering to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
