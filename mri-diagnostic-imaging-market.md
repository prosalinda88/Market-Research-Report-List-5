<p><p>MRI Diagnostic Imaging Market includes two main types: Open MRI and Closed MRI. Open MRI machines have a more spacious design, making them ideal for patients who may feel claustrophobic or anxious during imaging procedures. Closed MRI machines have a tunnel-like design and produce higher resolution images, making them more suitable for patients who require detailed diagnostic information. Both types of MRI machines play a crucial role in healthcare by providing accurate and precise imaging for various medical conditions.</p></p>
