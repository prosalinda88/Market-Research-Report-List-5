<p><p>The NFC Antenna Module market includes two main types: One-way Transmission and Arbitrary Direction. One-way Transmission involves communication between two devices in a specific direction. On the other hand, Arbitrary Direction allows for communication between devices in any orientation. Both types serve different purposes depending on the specific application, with One-way Transmission offering more focused communication and Arbitrary Direction providing more flexibility in device placement and orientation. These variations cater to a diverse range of NFC antenna module market needs.</p></p>
