<p><p>Pre-filled syringes are available in two main types: glass pre-filled syringes and plastic pre-filled syringes. Glass pre-filled syringes are commonly used for drugs that are sensitive to interactions with plastic, and are known for their high chemical resistance and inert nature. Plastic pre-filled syringes, on the other hand, are preferred for their cost-effectiveness and reduced risk of breakage. Both types offer convenience, accuracy, and ease of use, making them popular choices in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
