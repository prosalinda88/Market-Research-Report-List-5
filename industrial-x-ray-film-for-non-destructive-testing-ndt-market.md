<p><p>Industrial X-ray film for Non-destructive Testing (NDT) is available in two main types - Non-Screen Type Films and Screen Type Films. Non-Screen Type Films do not require the use of an intensifying screen and are commonly used for high-resolution imaging. On the other hand, Screen Type Films are designed to be used with an intensifying screen to enhance image quality and sensitivity. Both types of films are essential tools for conducting NDT inspections in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
