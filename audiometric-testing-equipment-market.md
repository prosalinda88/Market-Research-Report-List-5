<p><ul><li>Screening Audiometry</li><li>Diagnostic Audiometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audiometric testing equipment is divided into two main types: Screening Audiometry and Diagnostic Audiometry. Screening Audiometry is used to quickly assess a large number of individuals for hearing issues, such as during routine check-ups or in mass screening programs. On the other hand, Diagnostic Audiometry is more in-depth and is used by healthcare professionals to accurately diagnose and evaluate hearing disorders in individuals who have failed screening tests or are experiencing symptoms of hearing loss.</p></p>
