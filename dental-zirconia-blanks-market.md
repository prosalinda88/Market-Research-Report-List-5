<p><p>Dental Zirconia Blanks are categorized into two main types: Monolayer Zirconia Blanks and Multilayer Zirconia Blanks. Monolayer Zirconia Blanks are made from a single layer of zirconia material, offering high strength and durability. On the other hand, Multilayer Zirconia Blanks consist of multiple layers of zirconia with varying translucency levels, allowing for a more natural-looking appearance. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the dental restoration being created.</p></p>
