<p><p>System-in-Package (SiP) technology encompasses various market types including 2-D IC Packaging, 2.5-D IC Packaging, 3-D IC Packaging, and Multifunctional Substrate Integrated Component Package. 2-D IC Packaging involves stacking multiple ICs on a single substrate, while 2.5-D IC Packaging involves placing multiple ICs on an interposer. 3-D IC Packaging involves stacking ICs vertically to increase functionality and performance. Multifunctional Substrate Integrated Component Package combines various functions such as ICs, passives, and antennas on a single substrate for compact and efficient designs.</p></p>
