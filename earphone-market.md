<p><ul><li>Dynamic</li><li>Electrostatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The earphone market can be broadly categorized into two types: dynamic and electrostatic. Dynamic earphones use a traditional coil and magnet system to generate sound, providing a dynamic and impactful listening experience. On the other hand, electrostatic earphones use a more advanced technology that involves a thin, electrically charged diaphragm to produce sound with greater detail and clarity. Both types have their unique advantages and cater to different preferences and audiophile needs in the market.</p></p>
