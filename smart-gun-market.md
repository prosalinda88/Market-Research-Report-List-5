<p><p>The smart gun market can be categorized into three main types: RFID, biometrics, and others. RFID technology uses radio frequency electromagnetic fields to identify and track tags attached to objects, enabling secure gun access. Biometric technology, on the other hand, uses unique physiological features such as fingerprints or facial recognition to authorize gun use. The "others" category includes alternative technologies like GPS tracking or personalized grips. Each type of smart gun market type offers unique solutions for enhancing gun safety and security.</p></p>
