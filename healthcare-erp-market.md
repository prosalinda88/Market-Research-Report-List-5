<p><p>Healthcare ERP market exists in two main types: On-premises and Cloud. On-premises systems require hardware and software to be installed locally on the healthcare organization's servers. This type offers more control and customization, but requires higher upfront costs. Cloud-based systems, on the other hand, are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. They offer lower initial costs, scalability, and flexibility. Both options have their own advantages and considerations, making it important for healthcare organizations to carefully assess their needs before choosing a solution.</p></p>
