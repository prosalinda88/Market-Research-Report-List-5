<p><p>Integrated labeling systems are used in various industries and can be classified into two main types: adhesive-based labeling and non-adhesive-based labeling markets. Adhesive-based labeling systems use adhesive labels that stick to the product or packaging, providing a secure and permanent attachment. On the other hand, non-adhesive-based labeling systems rely on methods like heat shrink sleeves or elastic bands to label products without the use of adhesives. Both market types offer efficient and reliable solutions for labeling products in different industries.</p></p>
