<p><p>Thermal Barrier Coatings Market consists of two main types - Metal/Alloy Coating and Ceramic Coating. Metal/Alloy Coating is a type of thermal barrier coating that is typically made up of metallic elements like nickel, cobalt, or iron. These coatings are known for their excellent thermal conductivity and mechanical properties. On the other hand, Ceramic Coatings are made up of ceramic materials like zirconia, alumina, or yttria-stabilized zirconia. They offer superior thermal insulation properties and are widely used in high-temperature applications.</p></p>
