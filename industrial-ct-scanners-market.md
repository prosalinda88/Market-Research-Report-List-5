<p><p>Industrial CT scanners are categorized into two main types: Micro Focus CT and High Energy CT. Micro Focus CT scanners are used for scanning smaller, more intricate objects with higher resolution and precision, making them ideal for industries like electronics and aerospace. On the other hand, High Energy CT scanners are designed for larger and denser objects that require higher penetration power, such as automotive and energy sectors. Both types offer advanced imaging capabilities and are vital tools for non-destructive testing and quality control in various industries.</p></p>
