<p><p>Car stamping is a process used in the automotive industry to shape and form metal sheets into desired parts for vehicles. Hot stamping involves heating the metal sheet before shaping it, resulting in parts that are stronger and lighter. Cold stamping, on the other hand, does not involve heating the metal sheet and is more commonly used for simpler shapes. Both hot and cold stamping processes are widely used in the automotive industry for manufacturing various components of vehicles.</p></p>
