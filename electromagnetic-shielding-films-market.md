<p><p>Electromagnetic shielding films are categorized into three main types based on the material used for shielding:</p><p>1. Conductive Adhesive Type: These films utilize conductive adhesives to provide electromagnetic shielding properties. They are flexible and easy to use, making them suitable for various applications.</p><p>2. Metal Alloy Type: These films are made of metal alloys such as copper, aluminum, or nickel. They offer high shielding effectiveness and durability, making them ideal for demanding electromagnetic shielding applications.</p><p>3. Metal Microneedle Type: These films contain tiny metal microneedles that create a high-density shielding barrier. They provide excellent performance in blocking electromagnetic interference and are commonly used in advanced electronic devices.</p></p>
