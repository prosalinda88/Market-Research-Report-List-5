<p><p>Pneumatic clutches are a type of mechanical device used in various industries to engage and disengage power transmission between two rotating shafts. There are two main types of pneumatic clutches: Friction type and Toothed type. Friction type clutches rely on the friction between the clutch plate and pressure plate to engage the transmission, while toothed type clutches use gear teeth to engage the transmission. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on their specific characteristics and requirements.</p></p>
