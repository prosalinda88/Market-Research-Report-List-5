<p><p>Wear resistant steel market can be categorized into three main types based on hardness levels: Under HBW 400, HBW 400-500, and Above HBW 500. Under HBW 400 refers to steel with a hardness level below 400 on the Brinell hardness scale, offering basic wear resistance. HBW 400-500 includes steel with a hardness range between 400 and 500, providing moderate wear resistance. Above HBW 500 consists of steel with a hardness level higher than 500, offering superior wear resistance for demanding industrial applications.</p></p>
