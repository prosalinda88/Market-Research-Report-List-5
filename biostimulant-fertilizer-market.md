<p><p>Biostimulant fertilizer market is comprised of different types, including Acid Based and Plant Extract forms. On the other hand, Plant Extract biostimulant fertilizers are derived from natural sources like seaweed, algae, and other plant extracts, providing plants with beneficial growth hormones and nutrients. Both types aim to enhance plant health, yield, and overall productivity in agriculture.</p></p>
