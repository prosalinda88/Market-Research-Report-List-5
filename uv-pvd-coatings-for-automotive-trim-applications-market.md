<p><p>UV PVD coatings for automotive trim applications typically consist of three main types: UV base-coat, UV mid-coat, and UV top-coat. The UV base-coat is applied as the initial layer to provide adhesion to the substrate. The UV mid-coat serves as the main barrier against environmental factors and wear and tear. The UV top-coat is the final layer applied for added protection and aesthetic appeal. Each layer plays a crucial role in enhancing the durability and appearance of automotive trim components.</p></p>
