<p><p>The Automotive Air Conditioning System (A & C) Parts market can be divided into two main types based on the type of controlling device used: Orifice Tube System Type A/C and Expansion Valve System Type A/C. The Orifice Tube System relies on a fixed orifice to control refrigerant flow, making it a simpler and more cost-effective option. On the other hand, the Expansion Valve System uses a valve to regulate the flow of refrigerant, providing better control over cooling capacity and efficiency. Both systems are widely used in the automotive industry but cater to different needs and budgets.</p></p>
