<p><p>IPS display technology has various types in the market, including AH-IPS, E-IPS, H-IPS, and PLS. AH-IPS (Advanced High-Performance In-Plane Switching) offers improved color accuracy and wider viewing angles. E-IPS (Enhanced In-Plane Switching) provides enhanced image quality and energy efficiency. H-IPS (High-Performance In-Plane Switching) offers even better color accuracy and viewing angles than AH-IPS. PLS (Plane-to-Line Switching) is a similar technology to IPS with excellent color reproduction and viewing angles. These different types cater to various consumer needs and preferences in the display market.</p></p>
