<p><ul><li>Immunochemistry</li><li>Molecular Diagnostics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IVD infectious diseases market includes two main types of diagnostic technologies: immunochemistry and molecular diagnostics. Immunochemistry involves the detection of infectious diseases through the use of antibodies or antigens. This method is commonly used for rapid screening tests. On the other hand, molecular diagnostics relies on the detection of nucleic acids to identify infectious agents at a genetic level. This technology is highly accurate and is often used for detecting antibiotic-resistant strains and emerging pathogens. Both immunochemistry and molecular diagnostics play a crucial role in diagnosing infectious diseases.</p></p>
