<p><p>Polyester Composite Geogrid is commonly available in two market types: Biaxial Tension and Uniaxial Tension. Biaxial Tension geogrids are designed to handle tensile forces in both longitudinal and transverse directions, making them suitable for applications where stress is exerted from multiple directions. On the other hand, Uniaxial Tension geogrids are primarily used for applications where the main force is exerted in one direction, offering excellent tensile strength and reinforcement capabilities. Both variations of geogrids play a vital role in providing stability and enhancing the performance of civil engineering projects.</p></p>
