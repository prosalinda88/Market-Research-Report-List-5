<p><p>Shelf life testing is conducted through two main methods - Real Time and Accelerated. Real Time testing involves monitoring the actual shelf life of a product by storing it under normal conditions and observing how long it takes for the product to deteriorate. On the other hand, Accelerated testing involves subjecting the product to extreme conditions such as high temperature, humidity, or light to speed up the deterioration process and predict its shelf life in a shorter time frame. Both methods are important for manufacturers to ensure product quality and safety.</p></p>
