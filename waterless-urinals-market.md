<p><p>Waterless urinals market includes different types of products such as liquid sealant cartridges, membrane traps, biological blocks, and mechanical balls. Liquid sealant cartridges use a liquid barrier to trap odors and prevent it from entering the restroom. Membrane traps use a flexible membrane to block odors while allowing liquid to pass through. Biological blocks contain beneficial bacteria that break down odors and prevent build-up of waste. Mechanical balls physically trap odors and prevent them from escaping. Each type of product offers a unique solution for odor control in waterless urinals.</p></p>
