<p><ul><li>Dispersed Inks</li><li>Reactive Inks</li><li>Acid Inks</li><li>Pigment Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital textile printing inks are categorized into four main types: dispersed inks, reactive inks, acid inks, and pigment inks. Dispersed inks are ideal for polyester fabrics, reactive inks are used for natural fibers like cotton, acid inks work well on silk and nylon, while pigment inks are versatile and can be used on a variety of fabrics. Each type of ink offers unique properties and benefits, catering to different printing needs and fabric types in the digital textile printing market.</p></p>
