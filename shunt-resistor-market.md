<p><p>Shunt resistor market consists of two main types: SMD resistor and Through Hole resistor. SMD resistors are surface-mounted devices that are smaller in size and ideal for high-density circuit boards. Through Hole resistors are larger and mounted through holes on the circuit board for more robust construction. Both types serve the same purpose of measuring current by providing a small voltage drop in a circuit. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, making them suitable for different applications based on size, power rating, and precision requirements.</p></p>
