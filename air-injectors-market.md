<p><p>Air injectors are devices that inject air into an engine's combustion chamber to improve fuel combustion and reduce emissions. There are two main market types for air injectors: Multi-Port Injectors and Single-Port Injectors. Multi-Port Injectors deliver air to multiple points in the combustion chamber, resulting in more efficient combustion. On the other hand, Single-Port Injectors deliver air to a single point in the combustion chamber. Both types are essential in optimizing the performance and environmental impact of internal combustion engines.</p></p>
