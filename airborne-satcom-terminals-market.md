The Ka-band market for Airborne SATCOM terminals utilizes high frequency signals to enable high-speed data transmission, suitable for bandwidth-intensive applications. Both types of terminals serve the same purpose of enabling real-time communication in the air, but differ in their operating frequencies and capabilities.</p></p>
