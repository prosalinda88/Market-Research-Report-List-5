<p><p>Silicon-based fingerprint sensors are primarily categorized into two types: touch type and slide type. Touch type sensors require the user to place their finger directly on the sensor to capture the fingerprint, while slide type sensors require the user to slide their finger across the sensor to read the fingerprint. Touch type sensors are typically more commonly used in smartphones and other handheld devices, while slide type sensors are often found in access control systems and other security applications that require high levels of accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
