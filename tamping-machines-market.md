<p><ul><li>Straight Track Tamping Machines</li><li>Points and Crossing Tamping Machines</li><li>Multi-purpose Tamping Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tamping machines market can be categorized into three types based on their functionality: straight track tamping machines, points and crossing tamping machines, and multi-purpose tamping machines. Straight track tamping machines are designed specifically for maintaining the straight track alignment. Points and crossing tamping machines are used for maintaining the alignment and level at track junctions. Multi-purpose tamping machines are versatile and can be used for various tamping tasks on different types of tracks. Each type caters to specific needs of the railway maintenance industry.</p></p>
