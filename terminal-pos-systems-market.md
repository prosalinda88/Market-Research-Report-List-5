<p><p>Terminal POS systems can be divided into two main types: fixed type and mobile type. Fixed type systems are stationary and typically used in traditional brick-and-mortar retail stores. They consist of a POS terminal connected to a central server to process transactions. On the other hand, mobile type systems are portable and can be used on-the-go, allowing businesses to accept payments anywhere. These systems typically consist of a mobile device such as a tablet or smartphone with POS software installed.</p></p>
