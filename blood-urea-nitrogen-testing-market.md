<p><p>Blood Urea Nitrogen (BUN) testing is a widely used blood test that measures the amount of nitrogen in the blood that comes from urea, a waste product of protein metabolism. BUN testing is often used to assess kidney function, as elevated levels may indicate impaired kidney function. This test is commonly performed as part of a basic metabolic panel during routine health check-ups or to monitor kidney function in patients with chronic kidney disease, dehydration, or congestive heart failure.</p><p>The Blood Urea Nitrogen Testing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.3% during the forecast period.
