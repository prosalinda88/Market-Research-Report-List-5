<p><p>Heat shrinkable tubes are categorized into different types based on the materials used in their construction. Polyolefin heat shrinkable tubes are the most common type and offer good electrical insulation and resistance to chemicals. Fluoropolymer heat shrinkable tubes provide exceptional heat resistance and are often used in high-temperature applications. Chlorinated polyolefin heat shrinkable tubes have superior mechanical strength and flame retardant properties. Other types of heat shrinkable tubes may include different materials or specialized characteristics to meet specific application requirements.</p></p>
