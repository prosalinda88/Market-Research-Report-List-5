<p><p>CO2 heat pumps come in different types to cater to various heating needs. Air to Water Monobloc systems are compact units that contain all components in one outdoor unit. Air to Water Cylinder systems utilize a separate indoor hot water cylinder for heat exchange. Air to Water Split systems have an outdoor unit and indoor unit for more flexibility. Exhaust Air systems extract heat from indoor air, providing efficient heating. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of installation, efficiency, and performance.</p></p>
