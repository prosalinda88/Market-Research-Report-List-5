<p><p>The Engine Exhaust System Muffler market consists of two main types: Absorption Exhaust Mufflers and Reactive Exhaust Mufflers. Absorption Mufflers use sound-absorbing materials to reduce noise levels, while Reactive Mufflers use chambers and tubes to create sound waves that cancel out noise. Absorption Mufflers are effective in reducing high-frequency noise, while Reactive Mufflers are more efficient at reducing low-frequency noise. Both types of mufflers play a crucial role in reducing pollution and noise levels from engine exhaust systems.</p></p>
