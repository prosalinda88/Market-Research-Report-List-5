<p><ul><li>Double Rail</li><li>Single Rail</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Warehouse guard rails come in two main types: double rail and single rail. Double rail guard rails provide maximum protection with two parallel rails running horizontally to protect against impacts from multiple directions. Single rail guard rails offer a more cost-effective option with a single rail running horizontally to prevent impacts in one direction. Both types of guard rails are essential for safeguarding warehouse personnel, equipment, and inventory from accidental collisions and falls.</p></p>
