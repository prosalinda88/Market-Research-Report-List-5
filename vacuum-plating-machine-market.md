<p><ul><li>Suspension Type</li><li>Roller Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum Plating Machine Market offers two main types: Suspension Type and Roller Type. Suspension Type machines suspend the items to be plated in a vacuum chamber, allowing for thorough and uniform coating. On the other hand, Roller Type machines use rollers to move the items through the chamber for coating. Suspension Type machines are ideal for delicate or intricate objects, while Roller Type machines are better suited for mass production of larger items. Both types offer efficient and effective plating solutions for various industries.</p></p>
