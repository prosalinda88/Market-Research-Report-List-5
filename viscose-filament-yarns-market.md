<p><p>Viscose filament yarns are available in continuous and semi-continuous market types. Continuous filament yarns are produced in a continuous process, creating long, continuous strands of yarn without any breaks. These yarns are known for their strength and smooth texture, making them ideal for applications where durability is important. Semi-continuous filament yarns are produced in shorter lengths with occasional breaks in the yarn. While not as strong as continuous yarns, semi-continuous yarns are more flexible and easily manipulated, making them suitable for a variety of uses.</p></p>
