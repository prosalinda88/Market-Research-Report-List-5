<p><p>Liquid particle counting systems are used to detect and monitor the presence of particles in liquids. There are two main types of markets for these systems - offline type and in-line remote type. Offline type systems involve taking samples of the liquid and analyzing them separately from the main process. In-line remote type systems, on the other hand, continuously monitor the liquid in real-time without the need for manual sampling. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and cleanliness of liquids in various industries.</p></p>
