<p><p>TDF Flange Forming Machines come in two main market types: Simplex and Duplex. Simplex machines are designed for single-duct production and are typically more compact and cost-effective. On the other hand, Duplex machines are capable of forming flanges on two ducts simultaneously, increasing productivity and efficiency. Both types of machines offer different advantages depending on the production needs of the user, with Simplex machines being more suitable for smaller-scale operations and Duplex machines for larger-scale production.</p></p>
