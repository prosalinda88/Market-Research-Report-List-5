<p><ul><li>Mechanical Purging</li><li>Chemical/Foaming Purging</li><li>Liquid Purging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoplastic purging compounds are used in various industries to clean injection molding machines and extruders. These compounds can be classified into three main types: mechanical purging, chemical/foaming purging, and liquid purging. Mechanical purging involves using abrasive materials to physically remove residue from equipment. Chemical/foaming purging involves using chemical compounds that expand and push out contaminants. Liquid purging involves flushing out residues with a liquid solution. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in specific applications based on the cleaning requirements.</p></p>
