<p><p>Flame Retardant Polyester Staple Fiber (FRP) can be categorized into two main types: Inherent FRP Staple Fiber and Treated FRP Staple Fiber. Inherent FRP Staple Fiber is inherently flame retardant due to the composition of the fiber itself, while Treated FRP Staple Fiber is treated with chemical additives to enhance its flame retardant properties. Both types offer fire protection for various applications such as textiles, furniture, and automotive components. The choice between the two types depends on specific requirements and performance characteristics needed for the end product.</p></p>
