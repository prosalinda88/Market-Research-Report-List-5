<p><p>Medical Coding Service market consists of two main types: International Classification of Diseases (ICD) and Healthcare Common Procedure Code System (HCPCS). ICD is a standardized system for classifying diseases, injuries, and causes of death worldwide, providing a common language for reporting and monitoring health conditions. HCPCS, on the other hand, is used primarily in the United States to represent procedures and services performed by healthcare providers. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring accurate reimbursement and data analysis in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
