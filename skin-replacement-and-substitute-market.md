<p><p>Skin replacement and substitute market types include allografts, xenografts, cultured epithelial autograft, collagen scaffolds, acellular dermal matrix, amniotic membrane, and synthetic skin. Allografts are skin grafts from a donor of the same species, xenografts are from a different species, and cultured epithelial autografts are grown from a patient's own cells. Collagen scaffolds, acellular dermal matrix, and amniotic membrane are used as skin substitutes. Synthetic skin is made from artificial materials for skin grafts and wound healing.</p></p>
