<p><p>Bacterial endotoxin testing is crucial in various industries like pharmaceuticals and medical devices to ensure product safety. There are three main types of endotoxin tests: Gel Clot Endotoxin Test, Chromogenic Endotoxin Test, and Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test. The Gel Clot test involves the formation of a gel when endotoxins react with a specific protein. The Chromogenic test utilizes color changes to detect endotoxins, while the Turbidimetric test measures changes in turbidity to quantify endotoxin levels. Each test offers unique advantages for different applications.</p></p>
