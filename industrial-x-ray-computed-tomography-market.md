<p><p>Industrial X-ray Computed Tomography (CT) Market can be classified into two types: Line Beam Scanning and Cone Beam Scanning. Line Beam Scanning involves a fan-shaped X-ray beam moving across the object, providing detailed cross-sectional images. On the other hand, Cone Beam Scanning uses a cone-shaped X-ray beam to capture a 3D image of the object. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, electronics, and materials science for non-destructive testing and inspection purposes.</p></p>
