<p><p>Food Grade Liquid Nitrogen Market Types include cryogenic distillation and pressure swing adsorption. Cryogenic distillation involves the separation of gases based on boiling points, making it suitable for producing high purity liquid nitrogen. Pressure swing adsorption, on the other hand, relies on the differential adsorption of gases onto solid surfaces under pressure to separate nitrogen from air. Both methods play a crucial role in the production and distribution of food grade liquid nitrogen for various applications in the food industry.</p></p>
