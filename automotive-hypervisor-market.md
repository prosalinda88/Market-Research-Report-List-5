<p><strong>The Automotive Hypervisor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bare Metal Hypervisors</li><li>Hosted Hypervisors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive hypervisors can be classified into two main types: bare-metal hypervisors and hosted hypervisors. Bare-metal hypervisors run directly on the hardware of the automotive system, providing high performance and efficiency. Hosted hypervisors, on the other hand, run on top of an existing operating system, offering easier integration with other software but potentially sacrificing some performance. Both types of hypervisors play a crucial role in ensuring the secure and efficient operation of automotive systems.</p></p>
