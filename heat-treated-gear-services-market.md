<p><p>Heat treated gear services involve different types such as normalizing, quenching, tempering, and annealing. Normalizing heat treatment service involves heating the gear to a certain temperature to improve its mechanical properties. Quenching heat treatment service involves rapidly cooling the gear to increase its hardness. Tempering heat treatment service is used to reduce the brittleness of the gear after quenching. Annealing heat treatment service involves heating the gear to a specific temperature and then slowly cooling it to improve its machinability and reduce internal stresses.</p></p>
