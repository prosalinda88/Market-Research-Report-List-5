<p><ul><li>Fluid Economizers</li><li>Air-side Economizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial economizer markets can be broadly categorized into two types: fluid economizers and air-side economizers. Fluid economizers use a liquid, such as water or glycol, to transfer heat and improve energy efficiency in industrial processes. Air-side economizers, on the other hand, utilize outside air to cool a building or space, reducing the need for mechanical cooling systems. Both types of economizers play a crucial role in enhancing energy efficiency in industrial operations and reducing overall operational costs.</p></p>
