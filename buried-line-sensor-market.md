<p><p>A buried line sensor is a system that is used to detect underground cables, pipelines, and other buried infrastructure. The sensor works by emitting electromagnetic waves into the ground and detecting any changes in the signal that may indicate the presence of a buried line.</p><p>The Buried Line Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period.
