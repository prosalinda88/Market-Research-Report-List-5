<p><p>Automatic screen changers are devices used in plastics extrusion processes to automatically switch between screens, removing contaminants and ensuring continuous operation. There are two main types of automatic screen changers: Single Piston and Double Piston. Single Piston screen changers use a single hydraulic piston to switch screens, while Double Piston screen changers use two pistons for more efficient screen changes. Both types offer benefits such as reduced downtime, increased productivity, and improved product quality in extrusion processes.</p></p>
