<p><p>The Auto Night Vision System market is divided into two main types: Active System and Passive System. Active System uses infrared light to illuminate the road ahead, allowing the driver to see in the dark. Passive System, on the other hand, relies on ambient light sources like headlights to enhance visibility at night. Active Systems are more effective in complete darkness while Passive Systems are more cost-effective and require less maintenance. Both types aim to improve driver visibility and safety when driving in low-light conditions.</p></p>
