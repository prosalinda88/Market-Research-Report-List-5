<p><p>A Multi-beam Echo Sounder is a type of sonar technology used for underwater mapping and seabed profiling. It works by sending out multiple sonar beams in a fan shape, allowing for a wider area to be scanned compared to traditional single beam echo sounders. This results in higher resolution and more accurate data for various marine applications such as offshore oil and gas exploration, marine research, hydrographic surveying, and underwater construction.</p><p>The Multi-beam Echo Sounder Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period. Both types of systems are widely used in various applications including hydrographic surveying, marine exploration, and underwater mapping.</p></p>
